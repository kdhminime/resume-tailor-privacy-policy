Privacy Policy — Resume Tailor

Effective date: June 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Resume Tailor Chrome extension and its backend service collect, how we use and protect it, and the choices and rights you have. Resume Tailor rewrites your resume to match the job posting on the page you are viewing.

The short version. We collect only what is needed to tailor your resume: the resume content you provide, the job posting you choose to tailor against, and (optionally) an email address if you verify one to buy credits. All personal data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256-GCM). We never sell your data and never use it for advertising.

1. Who we are

This service is operated by Mosi ("we", "us", "our"), the provider and data controller for Resume Tailor. For privacy questions, data-access, or deletion requests, contact us using the details in the Contact us section below.

2. Data we collect and why

We collect the following categories of data, each only for the purpose stated.

DataWhat it isWhy we process it
Anonymous install token A random per-installation identifier generated automatically when you install the extension. It contains no personal information and requires no login. To associate your saved data and credit balance with your installation so the free tier works without an account.
Email address (optional) Collected only if you choose to verify an email via a one-time code. This is the only contact personal information we collect. To let you purchase credits and restore them across devices, and to send verification codes.
Resume / "fact base" Information you provide: name, contact details, work history, education, and skills. To generate tailored resumes for you.
Job postings The text (title, company, description) of the posting on your active browser tab — read only when you click "Tailor." Used as input to the tailoring so the result matches that role.
Tailored results The generated resume and assessment report. Stored in your history so you can revisit and reuse them.
Payment information Credits are purchased via Stripe. Card and payment details are handled entirely by Stripe — the app and our backend never see or store card numbers. To process purchases of generation credits.
Operational data Non-personal records such as generation counters, credit balance, timestamps, and which resume template was used. To operate the service, enforce credit limits, and show your usage.

We do not track your browsing history, and the extension does not run in the background on arbitrary pages.

3. How your data is stored and protected

  • Encrypted at rest. All personal data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it reaches our database — including resume content, job metadata, tailored results, and email.
  • No plaintext email. Email is stored as an encrypted value plus a one-way HMAC "blind index" (used only for uniqueness and lookup). No plaintext email column exists anywhere.
  • Hashed tokens. Account/installation tokens are stored hashed (SHA-256), never in plaintext.
  • Encrypted in transit. All traffic between the extension and our backend is over HTTPS.
  • Private database. Our PostgreSQL database has no public internet endpoint (private network) and is hosted on Microsoft Azure in the Canada Central region.

4. Third parties we share data with (subprocessors)

We share data only with the service providers below, and only to provide Resume Tailor to you. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — your resume content and the selected job posting are sent to Anthropic's API to generate the tailored resume. Under Anthropic's commercial API terms, API inputs are not used to train their models.
  • Stripe — payment processing for credit purchases.
  • Microsoft Azure — hosting of our backend and database, and transactional email (Azure Communication Services) used to send email-verification codes.

5. What the extension accesses on your device

The extension requests the minimum Chrome permissions needed to function:

  • activeTab + scripting — to read the visible job posting on your current tab, only when you click "Tailor." No content script runs on any page until you invoke "Tailor."
  • storage — to store the anonymous token, the backend-URL setting, and in-progress tailoring state locally on your device.

The extension does not read pages you do not act on, does not track browsing history, and does not run in the background on arbitrary pages.

6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as you keep Resume Tailor installed and have data stored with us, so the service remains usable. You can delete items from your history within the app, reset your identity in Settings (see below), or request full deletion at any time. When you request deletion of your account or data, we remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days and from encrypted backups within 90 days, except where we must retain limited records to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Reset identity. You can reset your identity in Settings, which clears the local anonymous token on your device.
  • Access & correction. You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and ask us to correct inaccuracies.
  • Deletion. You can request deletion of your stored data by emailing us at the address in the Contact us section. Please send your request from, or reference, the verified email or installation associated with the data so we can locate and verify it.

Our legal bases for processing are: performance of a contract (to provide the tailoring you request and process your purchases), your consent (for optional email verification), and our legitimate interests (to operate, secure, and improve the service). You may withdraw consent at any time, and you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Your data is processed and stored in Canada and may be transferred to service providers as described above; we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request access to and deletion of your personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's privacy

Resume Tailor is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Chrome Web Store Limited Use compliance

Our handling of user data complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, the data we access is used only to provide and improve the resume-tailoring features described to you; it is not transferred to third parties except as needed to provide the service (the subprocessors listed above), not sold, not used for advertising, and not used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes. Humans do not read your data except where required to provide the service, with your consent, for security purposes, or to comply with applicable law.
